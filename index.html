About Me

Hi, My name is Kuan-Yi, but I mostly go by Chloe. I was born and raised in Taipei, Taiwan. I lived in Austin, Texas for eight years and currently living in Downtown Los Angeles!

I started out as a web designer after getting my design degree, I needed to learn basic coding skill at the time in order to understand what the web is capable of and to communicate effectively with developers.

After dipping my toes into the web world, I absolutely love it! Being able to build my own ideas and design is just the greatest feeling in the world! And now I am finally taking my first step to learn coding at a professional level!

I am very excited for the learning opportunities and the challenges ahead!
